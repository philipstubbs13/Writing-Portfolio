About me

Ever since I was young, I have always been adventurous and loved to learn. As a kid, I liked challenging myself to explore new opportunities whether it be learning another language, learning how to play piano, trying out for the basketball team despite not being as tall as the other players, or striving to be one of the best chess players at school. I was not and am still not afraid to fail.

I always found different ways to solve problems whether it be figuring out how to solve a difficult problem in math class, how to be a couple moves ahead of my chess opponent, or how to be an efficient three point shooter on the basketball court. Exploration, determination, and strategic thinking have been and still are a big part of who I am. My passion to explore, tinker, fidget, and figure things out on my own is why I like to write.

I created this site to share my experiences, interests, and writing with you all. Visit the Portfolio page to see some of my writing projects. Feel free to explore, comment, provide feedback, and ask questions.
